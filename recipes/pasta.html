Pasta

Description

Creamy Veggie Cavatappi Pasta without Cream is loaded up with hidden vegetables, and it's so flavorful!

Ingredients

  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and cut into 3-inch pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • 1 (16 ounce) package cavatappi pasta

Steps

  1. Spread out tomatoes, carrots, onion, bell pepper, zucchini, and garlic on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning, salt, and crushed red pepper.
  2. Roast in the preheated oven until vegetables are soft, 30 to 35 minutes.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook cavatappi in the boiling water, stirring occasionally
  4. Toss pasta with vegetable sauce. Sprinkle with cheese and serve immediately.
