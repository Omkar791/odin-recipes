Chicken

Description

This chicken and quinoa recipe is incredibly easy, delicious, and spicy!Taste-test approved by a very picky 3-year-old. The fritters are coated with a thick sauce like spicy gravy curry made of corn flour. It is generally served with varieties of rice dishes like steamed rice, Chinese fried rice, Szechuan fried rice, as the main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, or as needed
  • 2 cloves garlic, or to taste, minced

Steps

  1. Season the chicken inside and out.
  2. Add the butter to the chicken and the pan.
  3. Stuff the cavity with celery.
  4. Bake until the chicken is fully roasted.
